The Bunny and the Wind

Once upon a time, there was a little white bunny who lived in the countryside and always looked up to the sky, admiring the wind that moved the flowers, spun the leaves, and flew everywhere...

His dream was to run as fast as the wind, reach the top of the mountain, and feel the strongest breeze in the world. But he was small, and everyone said it was impossible. Until one day, he believed in himself.

And you? Have you ever tried to run with the wind?
